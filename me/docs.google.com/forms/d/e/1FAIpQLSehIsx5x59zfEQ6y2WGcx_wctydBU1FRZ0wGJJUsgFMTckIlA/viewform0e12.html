Enquiry Form B.Tech. (CSE) AY 2026-27

B.Tech. Computer Science & Engineering program offered in collaboration with IBM is a ‘unique global’ initiative by the multinational IT Giant, to offer much needed applied information technology knowledge to graduating students, in various domain and futuristic technology areas for Indian & Foreign Meritorious Students. IBM’s cutting-edge skilling specialisation programs is delivered at LTSU in collaboration with National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), New Delhi.

Fill this form to stay connected and get the updates about the Registration Workflow for LTSU SET Entrance Exam for B.Tech. CSE

The exam Registration window will open in Feb. 2026

Eligibility Criteria

  • Unique program for meritorious students having minimum 60% marks in Class X & XII (Non Med.) AND scored at least 70 percentile in JEE/CUET/IBM Entrance Exam.

  • Admission of Foreign students as per UGC guidelines 

Admission Process

  • Online Registration.

  • Entrance Exam (CBT based).

  • Announcement of Results.

  • Interview and seat allocation with specialization as per university guidelines.

  • Provisional Admission Letter.

  • Seat confirmation after document verification and payment of Full Fee/Loan disbursement.

Exam Guidelines, Fee, Syllabus of the Examination & Dates of Exams -The Exam Schedule and details will be updated on the LTSU Punjab Website in the second week of Mar.2026.

Result Declaration - After the Entrance Exam, An online/offline Interview will be conducted for shortlisted candidates only with the shortlisted candidates, and based on the Test and Interview Performance final result of Selection, Branch allocation, Rejection or Waiting list will be informed to the candidates within 7 working days after Interview process.
