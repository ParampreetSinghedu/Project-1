Welcome to

Lamrin Tech Skills University Punjab

Lamrin has been recognised as a University "of" the industry, "for" the industry, and "by" the industry. Partnering with global tech giants to structure the curriculum, making students industry-ready by training in skill-based courses, mentored by leading industry experts and academicians, Lamrin aims at making India a leader in the skill-based workforce supply chain worldwide.

Lamrin Tech Skill University Punjab (LTSU) is established under Punjab State Act No 22 of 2021 and is recognised by UGC New Delhi under UGC Act 1956. LTSU is a first-of-its-kind industry-led Skill University in Punjab, India, supported by IBM and L&T as industry and skilling partners. Additionally, a UK-based NRI has contributed 100 acres of land along with a building for the university.

POWERED

by Industry & Skilling Partners

IBM India Private Limited (“IBM India”) has entered into an agreement with Lamrin Skills Tech University (LTSU, or the University), to facilitate the University in offering courseware and training to students of the University. Students are advised that enrolment in any courses offered by the University does not imply any preferential treatment in recruitment into IBM India. IBM does not make any representations or commitments, either to the University or to any students of the University, about student placements, salary commitments, or any other matters related to recruitment at IBM.

INTERNATIONAL

Collaborations that Pave the Way to an Extraordinary Global Career

Lamrin's collaboration with Globally Recognised Universities shall help train students under the finest mentors, alongside its own accomplished faculty. Guided and tutored by expert academicians and experienced professionals in international standards of technology, our overseas collaborations are designed in a way that ensures an all-around development of our students that captures the best of both worlds.


A place for EVERYONE to Dream

Lamrin offers its students distinctive Tech courses, which bridge the gap between skill-based higher education and employability. Our programs are conceptualised and designed in a way that equip you with critical knowledge in both the practical and theoretical realms to meet the required criteria for jobs that are potentially difficult to land. Our skill-based curriculum attends to developing an in-depth understanding of the subject matter that makes you ready when you step out of the campus and future-ready.

Lamrin's vocational training courses help upskill students for skill focused career. We support students in overcoming barriers in their career path by providing personalized guidance and training across multiple disciplines—even if they do not have a formal school education. Our model of integrated technical vocational training will make students employable to ascend the career ladder smoothly.
