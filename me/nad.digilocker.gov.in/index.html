Why NAD?

National Academic Depository (NAD) is born out of an initiative by MHRD to provide a 24X7 online depository to Academic institutions to store and publish their academic awards. The digital depository not only ensures easy access to and retrieval of an academic award but also validates and guarantees its authenticity and safe storage.

  • Crores of registered users
  • Legally valid documents
  • Instant verification
  • Globally accepted

Are you a student?

Students may access their degree/diploma certificates and marksheets via DigiLocker. In order to do so, they should register on DigiLocker.

Students may register on the ABC portal to verify the availability of credits in their ABC account. Upon registration, an APAAR ID will be created, which must be submitted to academic institutions so that credits may be sent into their respective ABC accounts.

Benefits of NAD

Aimed at the concept of paperless governance. It reduces the administrative overhead by minimizing the use of paper and curtailing the verification process. It is permanent and safe record keeping for all institutions. Students can access and transfer their awards easily. It also avoids the risk of forged certificates.

What's New on NAD?

We are pleased to announce that the APAAR ID creation module for foreign students is now live. Academic institutions can utilize this feature once the following steps are completed:

  1. Add the foreign user uploader from the user management module.
  2. Send an email to us requesting the addition of a verifier. By default, this role will be assigned to officials from Digital India Corporation (DIC).
  3. If an institution wishes to manage this process internally, please send a formal authorization letter with the following details of the concerned member:
    - Organization ID
    - Name
    - Designation
    - Mobile number associated with the DigiLocker account
    - Email ID
We look forward to a smooth implementation of this new feature.
