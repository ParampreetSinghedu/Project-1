About Educate the Educator (FDP)

Part of National Mission of Capabl & CSI

Is ChatGPT where your AI journey stops?
Maybe you’ve used it to draft a lesson plan or check grammar, and felt pretty good about it.
But that’s just a fraction of what AI can actually do to transform your teaching, research, and daily academic tasks.

Capabl, in collaboration with the Computer Society of India, is on a mission to upskill 10,000 faculty members in Agentic AI by 2026 — completely free of cost. This FDP is your first step toward becoming part of that movement.

This fully-sponsored, 1-day, offline Faculty Development Program (FDP) is designed to help educators move from using AI to creating with it.

Through hands-on learning, you'll discover how to integrate Large Language Models (LLMs), and Agentic AI systems into your academic work, without needing to code.

What You’ll Learn To Do

In one day, you’ll explore how AI can simplify and elevate your work as an educator:
  • Experience powerful AI tools that simplify your day-to-day teaching
  • Build 'Master Ji', your first Conversational AI Agent
  • Discover how LLMs (like ChatGPT, Gemini) actually work
  • Understand and fix AI hallucinations in classroom contexts
  • Explore topics like RAGs, Vector Search and Agentic AI
  • Participate in a fun Hackathon, where you’ll brainstorm ideas and build things

Tools You'll Use

These are not apps for techies — they’re made for teachers, just like you:
Turn research papers into bite-sized notes
Turn complex ideas into crystal-clear insights.
Create concept visuals (e.g., “solar cell working”) instantly
Turn repetitive tasks into automated workflows.
