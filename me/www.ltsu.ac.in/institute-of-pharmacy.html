About Us

Lamrin has been recognised as a University “of” the industry, “for” the industry, and “by” the industry. Partnering with global tech giants to structure the curriculum, making students industry-ready by training in skill-based courses, mentored by leading industry experts and academicians, Lamrin aims at making India a leader in the skill-based workforce supply chain worldwide.

Rayat Institute of Pharmacy is offering D.Pharmacy, B.Pharmacy and M.Pharmacy courses in the discipline of Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutics & Pharmacology. The institute is affiliated to PCI and Lamrin Tech Skills University, Punjab.All the courses are approved by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) & Punjab State Board of Technical Education (PSBTE & IT). The institute continuously strives to provide high quality professional education and research training to students with the help of their ever dedicated staff to build one of the best educational setups.

B.Pharmacy

  • Duration- 4 years

  • Eligibility- Candidate shall have passed 10+2 examination conducted by the respective state/central government authorities recognized as equivalent to 10+2 examination by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) with English as one of the subjects and Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (P.C.M) and or Biology (P.C.B /P.C.M.B.) as optional subjects individually. Any other qualification approved by the Pharmacy Council of India as equivalent to any of the above examinations


B. Pharm Lateral Entry

  • Duration- 3 years

  • Eligibility- A pass in D. Pharm. course from an institution approved by the Pharmacy Council of India under section 12 of the Pharmacy Act


D.Pharmacy

  • Duration- 2 years

  • Eligibility- Minimum qualification for admission to Diploma in Pharmacy-A pass in 10+2 examination (science academic stream) with Physics, Chemistry and Biology or Mathematics or Any other qualification approved by the Pharmacy Council of India as equivalent to the above examination.


M.Pharmacy Master of Pharmacy (M. Pharm)

  • Specifications-

    1. Pharmaceutics
    2. Pharmaceutical Chemistry
    3. Pharmacology

  • Duration- 2 years

  • Eligibility- Bachelor of Pharmacy

About Us

Rayat Institute of Pharmacy (RIP) was established in 2003 with Degree & Diploma courses in Pharmacy programs and is also now offering Master courses in the discipline of Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutics & Pharmacology. The institute was affiliated to Punjab Technical University, Jalandhar and currently runs under Lamrin Tech Skills University and the courses are approved by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), All India Council of Technical Education , & Punjab State Board of Technical Education (PSBTE). The institute has successfully completed 17 batches at bachelor level, 15 batches at master level & 15 batches at Diploma level with 100% placement. From the last 20 years, we are on a journey of continuous growth and we boast a big swarm of students. The Institute continuously strives to provide high quality professional education and research training to students with the help of their ever dedicated staff to build one of the greatest educational set ups.

Rayat Institute of Pharmacy provides ever-challenging and supportive learning environment. The Institute has a team of competent and dynamic faculties with varied backgrounds from research, industry and academia. The Faculty is actively involved in various research activities either independently or through research projects of PG students. A team of technical supporting staff is also very experienced, efficient and hardworking. We are committed to educate and empower professional, graduate and post-graduate students to be highly qualified pharmacy practitioners, scientists and educators.

Labs & Infrastructure

Lecture Halls: The institute has ten well-furnished seminar-cum-lecture halls equipped with modern pedagogical techniques for effective teaching and learning.

Laboratories: The RIP has 25 well equipped and spacious research laboratory equipped with state of art facilities required for each specialized subject of pharmaceutical sciences including Central Instrument Room (has modern sophisticated instruments) and Computer Lab equipped with P-IV configuration on LAN for the students and faculty. The institute has open Wi-Fi internet facility.

Library: RIP has a well-established library which caters to the needs of the students and faculty. It is automated with a wide collection of latest books, periodicals and DVDs which is expanded and updated each year.

Centrally e-Library/DELNET facility is available with around 69 soft copies of various books.

Animal House: The Institute has a large animal house with all the facilities for the maintenance and performance of animal experimentation. The animal house facility is registered with the Committee for the purpose of Control and Supervision of Experiments on Animals (CPCSEA),   New Delhi.  The Institute has its own Institutional Animal Ethical Committee which scrutinizes all the research proposals of PG students and routinely inspects the animal house.
