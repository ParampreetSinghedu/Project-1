About Us

Lamrin has been recognised as a University “of” the industry, “for” the industry, and “by” the industry. Partnering with global tech giants to structure the curriculum, making students industry-ready by training in skill-based courses, mentored by leading industry experts and academicians, Lamrin aims at making India a leader in the skill-based workforce supply chain worldwide.

University School of Commerce and Management

  • Home
  • University School of Commerce and Management
  • Centre for Excellence (CoE)
  • Apply Now

University School of Commerce & Management aims to impart interdisciplinary and multi-disciplinary knowledge to students across widely different fields. The School provides a rich learning environment with world class infrastructure together with top class faculty team to develop and hone the managerial and leadership abilities of its students through a rich portfolio of pedagogical tools. It endeavors to create a well-groomed, one of the best citizens using an academic environment that strengthens  their professional aptness.

At University School of Commerce and Management (USCM), we believe that the success of the school comes from the achievement of its students. To ensure that our students succeed in all their endeavors, USCM has created a rich knowledge resource pool. The department takes pride in nurturing future managers with acumen and intellect to lead professional life with integrity. There are highly experienced teaching staff and highly innovative tracking pedagogy.

Highlights

  • Industry-driven curriculum and pedagogy.

  • Professional Development / Soft Skill Development/ Communication skills are part of the curriculum.

  • Industry Projects, Internships, industry visits, guest lecture, workshops, seminars, cultural activities, competitions and relevant outreach activities.

  • Scholarships available to the meritorious students

Dr Ashutosh Sharma

drashutosh@ltsu.ac.in

It gives me immense pride to welcome you to the University School of Commerce and Management at Lamrin Tech Skills University Punjab. Our mission is to nurture future leaders who combine academic excellence with practical skills, innovation and ethical values.
At our school, we emphasize a holistic approach to education—integrating rigorous classroom learning with hands-on industry exposure, research opportunities, and entrepreneurial development. We are committed to preparing our students not only for successful careers but also for meaningful contributions to society.

We believe that Commerce and Management are not just disciplines of study, but powerful tools to shape economies, empower communities and drive sustainable growth. With a qualified & dedicated faculty team, modern infrastructure and industry collaborations, we ensure that every student receives the guidance and resources needed to thrive in today’s dynamic global environment.

I invite you to explore our programs, engage with our initiatives and become part of a vibrant academic community that values knowledge, innovation, and integrity. Together, let us build a future where education transforms lives and leadership inspires change.



Objectives

  • To promote an understanding of global emerging trends in business and management scenarios.

  • To develop the ability of self-assessment and motivate others in achieving organizational objectives.

  • To develop the ability to understand and apply a multi-dimensional approach to achieve the overall mission of the organization.

  • To develop skills in business analysis and market analysis

  • To impart ethical and moral values for social well-being.


Vision of USCM

To become the premier and leading management school by developing leading and analytical skills in students by providing them business education incorporated with ethics and cultural values to serves the nation and undertaking research towards national and international issues.


Mission of USCM

  • To develop leadership, intellectual learning, managerial and analytical skills in students.

  • Making students responsible by providing them education to global standards.

  • To instil and nurture sense of ethics and values in students.

  • To promote and undertake all-inclusive research and development.
